Timbrel Leadership Team

Bishop PJ Edmund, Sr.

Bishop Peter John Edmund, Sr., is the sixteen-year founder and establishmentarian of Timbrel Church International. A native of Bristol, England in the United Kingdom, he has matured his ministry and pastorate throughout the United States, the United Kingdom, Jamaica and Canada. With over 30 years’ experience in the Christian ministry and over 20 years in Higher Education he holds an earned doctoral degree in Philosophy, Christian Counseling and Psychology, two Honorary Doctorates in Divinity and Humane Letters, a Master’s degrees in Psychology and Theology/Cornerstone University/FL and Bachelor’s degree in Chemistry/Morgan State University. He currently works for Timbrel Church International, Inc. and PJ Edmund Ministries (a faith-based and minority small business owner) and has extensive experience in non-profit management, fund raising and community development. Bishop PJ is the Campus President for the Maryland Campus of Cornerstone Christian University, the Executive Director of Timbrel Community Development Corporation, Inc., and the Director of PJ Edmund Ministries, a global ministry committed to presenting the gospel.

Executive Pastor Chelly Edmund

Executive Pastor Michelle Edmund has served the vision of Timbrel Church International for the past sixteen years and oversees one of three councils but is specifically the pastor over the SALT Youth and Young Adult Fellowship. With a degree in Early Childhood Development from Stevenson University, MD and an Honorary Bachelor’s degree in Theology/Cornerstone Christian University. With a further background in Liability insurance and education, Executive Pastor Chelly has over 13 years’ experience in education administration and currently works full time for the DC Bar as a Senior Legal Administrative Assistant. In addition, she serves as the Vice President of the Board of Trustees for Timbrel Church International, Inc., and a board member on the Board of Timbrel Ministries International, Inc., and has over 20 years’ experience in ministry.

Associate Pastor C. Lisa Fields

Associate Pastor C. Lisa Fields has served the vision of Timbrel Church International, Inc., for over five years but has celebrated over thirty years in ministry and has been involved in Leadership and Ministerial Training and Development as part of her service to the local church and body of Christ. Pastor Fields oversees one of three council but more specifically oversees and ministers to the Women of Substance Fellowship as well as overseeing the Finance Ministry. She currently serves as the Vice President and Dean of Students of the Maryland Campus of Cornerstone Christian University and a chief facilitator/lecturer with Pneuma Fellowship Institute which speaks to the training and development of ministers in the areas of licensing and credentials. Pastor Fields holds a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration/Morgan State University/MD, a Bachelor’s Degree in Theology/North Carolina School of Theology and is pursuing a Master’s degree in Theology with Cornerstone Christian University. With over 40 year’s experience in Business Administration field she currently works for T Rowe Price as an Executive Assistant responsible for 5 Managers working in finance and budgets. She is a member of the Board of Trustees of Timbrel Church International, Inc., and Vice President of Timbrel Community Development Corporation, Inc.
